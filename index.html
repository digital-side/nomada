About us
Spanish Heritage, Reimagined.
Spanish Heritage, Reimagined.

At Nómada, the essence of Spanish gastronomy is transformed into a modern, innovative experience. Drawing from his extensive career alongside culinary greats like the Roca brothers, Heston Blumenthal, and Daniel Boulud and and with most recent employment 8 years served as Executive Chef of Binomio and Set of Six - Chef Landin presents a menu where every dish tells a story of Spain through a global lens.

While the full menu remains a surprise, diners can expect inventive dishes crafted with the finest ingredients, delivering bold and thought-provoking flavors. Each plate is an invitation to discover the limitless possibilities of Spanish cuisine, reimagined with a contemporary global flair.
